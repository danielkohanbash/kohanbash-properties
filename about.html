Daniel Kohanbash

Daniel is an alumni of the University of Southern California Marshal School of Business where he graduated with Honors receiving a B.S. in Business Administration with an emphasis in Real Estate Finance.
At a very young age, Daniel realized that Real Estate was his calling. His involvement, dates back to his childhood in which he would accompany his parents to open houses. Daniel can confidently admit that he has extensive knowledge of the Los Angeles Real Estate Market.

Daniel understands that buying or selling a home is more than just a transaction: it’s a life-changing experience. That’s why, Daniel is dedicated to providing exceptional, personalized service for all of his clients. He takes great pride in the relationships he builds and always works relentlessly on the client’s behalf to help them achieve their real estate goals.

Before Compass, Daniel worked at Keller Williams as a senior associate,Township Capital,Achieve Real Estate and Management and Marcus and Millichap.

Daniel’s past experience at Marcus and Millichap and his education, gives him the skills to not only look at a property from a homeowner standpoint but also, an investment standpoint. He understands the requirements needed from investors to make their ventures into a profitable return on investment. His experience coupled with his education, makes him an outstanding agent.

Daniel’s time out of the office are usually spent alongside family and friends, volunteering with individuals with special needs, and playing basketball.

Daniel's Promise To His Clients:

Daniel's philosophy is simple: clients come first. He pledges to be in constant communication with his clients, keeping them fully informed throughout the entire buying or selling process. He believes that if you’re not left with an amazing experience, he hasn't done his job. He doesn’t measure success through achievements or awards, but through the satisfaction of his clients.
